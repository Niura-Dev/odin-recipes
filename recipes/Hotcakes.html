Hotcakes


Ingredients

Servings: 2

  • 200 g whole milk
  • 20 g melted butter
  • 1 medium egg
  • 20 g white sugar
  • 75 g all-purpose flour
  • 6 g baking powder

Preparation

  1. 1. Mix 100 grams of whole milk, 20 grams of melted butter, and 1 medium egg in a bowl.
  2. 2. Add 20 grams of white sugar and mix well.
  3. 3. Next, add 75 grams of all-purpose flour and 6 grams of baking powder. Mix well until there are no lumps left and the batter is smooth and uniform.
  4. 4. Grease the pan with a little butter and place it over medium heat.
  5. 5. Pour 1/4 of the batter into the pan and let it cook over medium heat until the entire surface of the pancake is filled with bubbles.
  6. 6. Flip the batter and let the pancake cook for a couple of minutes on the other side.
  7. 7. Remove from the heat and repeat with the rest of the batter.
